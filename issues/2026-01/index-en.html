Section 4 · International Collaborations

Tanzania: A Promising Partner for RUDN

International cooperation is a key driver of citation growth. In September 2025, RUDN hosted a meeting with the Minister of Natural Resources and Tourism of the United Republic of Tanzania. Tanzania is among the actively developing scientific ecosystems in Africa: in 2025, scientists of the country published almost 4,000 papers in Scopus. Three main directions — ecology, natural resources, and tourism — correlate well with RUDN's research priorities, opening up specific opportunities for joint projects and grant applications.
